Privacy

This Privacy Policy explains how East Property (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you access or use our website [website URL], an online platform that aggregates and advertises real estate properties for sale and rent in the United Arab Emirates.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable UAE data protection laws, including Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL).

Description

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Preferred language
  • Property preferences
  • Enquiry messages and communications
  • Any other information you voluntarily submit via forms or contact request

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

1.3 Third-Party Information

  • Property details provided by developers, brokers, or agencies
  • Analytics data from service providers (e.g. website performance and traffic analysis)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the website
  • To display and manage property listings
  • To respond to enquiries and connect users with property sellers or agents
  • To improve user experience and website functionality
  • To send service-related communications
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of the platform

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests, provided such interests do not override your rights

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with:

  • Property developers, real estate agents, and brokers (only when you submit an enquiry)
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, IT support)
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

East Property does not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the UAE, East Property ensures that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UAE data protection laws to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

East Property retains personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

7. Your Rights

Under UAE data protection laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or updating of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Object to certain processing activities

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.
